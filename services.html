Cancellation Policy for Colette Webb Singing Tutoring

Effective Date: 28 September 2024

1. Cancellation Requests

You may cancel your singing lesson up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without incurring any fees. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the lesson may incur a cancellation fee.

2. Refund Policy

Full Refund: If you cancel your lesson at least 24 hours in advance, you are eligible for a full refund.

Partial Refund: Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the lesson will incur a 50% cancellation fee.

Non-Refundable: No refunds will be provided for lessons that have already commenced or completed.

3. Rescheduling

Rescheduling is encouraged over cancellation. Please contact me as soon as possible to arrange a new time.

4. Processing Time

Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after the cancellation request is approved.

5. Changes to This Policy

I reserve the right to modify this cancellation policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Cookie Policy for Colette Webb Singing Tutoring

Effective Date: 28 September 2024

At Colette Webb Singing Tutoring, we use cookies to improve your experience on our website and to understand how visitors interact with our site. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function more effectively and provide analytical insights into site usage.

2. Types of Cookies We Use
  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use the website, such as which pages are most popular.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your language preferences) to provide a more personalised experience.
3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to ensure our website functions properly, enhance your browsing experience, and analyse how users interact with our content.

4. Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to manage cookies through your browser settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies; however, please note that doing so may affect your user experience on our site.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics that set cookies on our behalf to help us understand how visitors interact with our site. These cookies are governed by the privacy policies of those third-party services.
