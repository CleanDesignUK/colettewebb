Lancashire Wellbeing Collective

The Lancashire Wellbeing Collective is a handpicked network of trusted wellbeing practitioners, coaches, and service providers from across Lancashire.

Each member of the collective is someone we know personally and trust to offer high-quality, compassionate support. Our aim is simple: to make it easier for people to access the right wellbeing services by connecting them with professionals who genuinely care.

Whether you’re looking for coaching, cold water therapy, wellbeing walks, or other holistic support, the providers listed here have been chosen because we believe in the value and integrity of their work.

This is not a paid directory or an open membership network—it’s a collaborative, community-focused initiative built on trust, shared values, and a commitment to supporting people’s well-being in Lancashire.

Cancellation Policy for Colette Webb Singing Tutoring

Effective Date: 28 September 2024

1. Cancellation Requests

You may cancel your singing lesson up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without incurring any fees. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the lesson may incur a cancellation fee.

2. Refund Policy

Full Refund: If you cancel your lesson at least 24 hours in advance, you are eligible for a full refund.

Partial Refund: Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the lesson will incur a 50% cancellation fee.

Non-Refundable: No refunds will be provided for lessons that have already commenced or completed.

3. Rescheduling

Rescheduling is encouraged over cancellation. Please contact me as soon as possible to arrange a new time.

4. Processing Time

Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after the cancellation request is approved.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use cookies to ensure our website functions properly, enhance your browsing experience, and analyse how users interact with our content.
