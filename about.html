Colette Webb Profesional Voice Coach and Singing Tutor

Meet Colette Webb

Hello! I’m Colette, an experienced voice coach, qualified teaching assistant and dedicated provider of private music lessons based in Lancaster. Music and education have always been at the heart of my life, serving as powerful tools for enhancing mental well-being and fostering meaningful social connections.

My journey in music commenced at a young age, but it was through my studies in community music that I truly recognised the transformative impact of singing. Observing how music can unite people, uplift spirits, and build confidence inspired me to establish a welcoming environment where individuals of all ages and skill levels can explore and develop their voices.

I adopt a student-centred approach, tailoring each session to align with my students' unique goals and abilities. My mission is clear: to help you find joy, confidence, and a sense of community through the enriching practice of singing.

My Experience

With extensive experience in voice coaching and private music lessons, I have worked with students of all ages and backgrounds. My career has spanned from leading choirs in community settings to conducting one-on-one tutoring sessions, where I have had the pleasure of helping individuals discover and refine their unique voices.

Highlights of my experience include:

Community Music Leader:

Completed a one-year mentorship with More Music, a community music charity, where I co-led and developed various group singing activities.

Singing Tutor at Music Spot:

Teaching at Music Spot since November 2023, providing expert voice coaching and private music lessons to students from diverse backgrounds.

Private Tutoring:

Over the past 15 months, I have been offering private music lessons, including to children with special needs, focusing on building confidence through personalised voice coaching.

Founder of Healing Harmonies:

Developing a singing-for-well-being group for young people, aimed at promoting mental health through the power of music and community singing.

My Qualifications

My commitment to voice coaching and music education is supported by a strong educational background and ongoing professional development:

Level 1 Safeguarding Training:

Ensuring a safe and supportive learning environment for all my students.

ABRSM Performance Vocal Diploma:

Currently preparing to further refine my vocal skills and knowledge through advanced training.

Vocal Health Course:

Studying vocal health to provide my students with the best techniques for maintaining a healthy and strong voice.

BA (Hons) in Music: Community Music:

Graduated with a 2:1, specialising in community music and researching the benefits of singing for mental well-being.

Grade 8 Singing Level (Merit):

My own vocal achievements have provided a solid foundation for my teaching methods.

Certified Youth Mental Health First Aider (MHFA England)

Certified Vocal Health First Aider (Vocal Health Education)

My Teaching Philosophy

I believe that voice coaching and private music lessons should be joyful and empowering experiences. My teaching style is warm, supportive, and tailored to each student's individual needs. Whether you're singing for pleasure, preparing for examinations, or using music as a pathway to well-being, I am here to guide you every step of the way.

Every voice is unique, and my goal is to create a safe space where students feel encouraged to explore and express themselves. I am passionate about using singing to build confidence, enhance social connections, and promote mental health.

Singing for Wellbeing

Singing has been proven to reduce anxiety, enhance mood, and foster a sense of community. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced vocalist, incorporating singing into your routine can significantly contribute to your mental and emotional wellbeing.

In this video, I explore various techniques and exercises that can help you harness the power of your voice to overcome anxiety and build confidence. Join me in this journey towards a healthier, happier you through the art of singing.

2. Types of Cookies We Use
